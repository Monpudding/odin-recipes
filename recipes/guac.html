Guacamole

Description

Quick, easy, and delicious guacamole! Add more salt, lime, or cilantro to your taste preference!

Ingredients:

  • 3 avocados, peeled and pitted
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  • 3 tablespoons of chopped cilantro
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper, or more to taste (optional)

Directions:

  1. Mash avocados, sea salt, garlic powder, and lime in a bowl
  2. Mix onion, tomatoes, and cilantro into avocado mix; season with cayenne pepper.
